Advocate Management System

The Advocate Management System is a robust platform designed to streamline the management of legal cases and client interactions. It allows users to easily add and view client information, ensuring quick access to essential details. The system also facilitates adding and viewing case stages, acts, and registered cases, providing a comprehensive overview of the legal process. With these functionalities, advocates can efficiently manage their practice.

Nursery Management System

The Nursery Management System project aimed to streamline the processes involved in managing various aspects of plant care and maintenance. Users could register and log in to access a user-friendly dashboard, where they could track plant health. The system featured a comprehensive database of plant species, complete with care instructions and growth tips.
